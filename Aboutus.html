About Us

Welcome to Stay Safe Madison, your trusted guide to staying safe in Madison, WI. Our mission is to provide up-to-date safety information and alerts to our community. Stay Safe Madison was founded in 2024 by a group of concerned citizens who wanted to make a difference in their local community. Our team is dedicated to gathering and disseminating the most accurate and helpful safety information available.
